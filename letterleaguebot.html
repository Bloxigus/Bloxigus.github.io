Things to note:

  • LetterLeagueBot does not know every word used by letter league. If a word comes up as invalid in the actual game, press the "Next Best Option" until a word does work

  • Press enter on a inserted word while hovering the grid to submit that word into the grid. Any words that have no point value assigned are not in the grid yet.

  • Wildcards (?) significantly increase the runtime of the program

  • Do not use this in an actual game, it ruins the fun winning by over 300 points
